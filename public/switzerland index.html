Top Most Beautiful places to visit in Switzerland

  • Lake Geneva – a beautiful crescent-shaped lake shared between France and Switzerland and overlooked by the Alps
  • Zurich – the stunning city nestled on a sprawling lake regularly recognised as one of the world’s most liveable cities
  • Lucerne and Lake Lucerne – a beautiful lakeside city surrounded by snowcapped mountains
  • Swiss National Park – part of the worldwide UNESCO Biosphere Reserve known for its stunning Alpine scenery
  • Chateau de Chillon – a beautiful and romantic castle nestled on Lake Geneva
